# Follow-Up Cadence

| Timing | Stage | Message Type |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| Day 5 | No reply to icebreaker | Gentle nudge |
| Day 7 | Product shipped | Tracking update |
| Day 10 | No reply after 2nd touch | Last gentle check-in |
| Day 14 | Product delivered (est.) | "How are you liking it?" |
| Day 21 | 1 week after delivery | Soft content ask |
| Day 30 | No content yet | Check-in |
| Day 45 | Still no content | Gentle close |

## Rules
1. **Max 5 follow-ups total** per creator
2. **Never follow up more than once every 5 days**
3. **Always warm tone**
4. After 45 days with no content: soft-close
