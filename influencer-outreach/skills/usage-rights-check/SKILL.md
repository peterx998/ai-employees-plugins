# Usage Rights & Compliance Check

| Right | Standard (Included) | Premium (Negotiate) |
|-------|--------------------|-------------------|
| Organic post | ✅ Included | — |
| Repost to brand account | ✅ 30 days | Extended perpetual |
| Spark Ads (TikTok) | Ask permission | Paid add-on |
| Whitelisting (Meta) | Ask permission | Paid add-on |
| Website/landing page use | Negotiate | Paid add-on |
| Paid ads (dark posts) | ❌ Not included | Paid add-on |

## Red Flags
- Creator asks for perpetual exclusive rights → escalate
- Creator wants to use competitor product in same video → decline
- Creator refuses to disclose paid partnership → escalate
- Medical claims in draft content → flag for review
