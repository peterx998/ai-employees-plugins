# Draft First Touch (Icebreaker)

Draft a personalized first outreach to a creator.

## Icebreaker Principles
1. **Personalize**: Reference 1-2 specific pieces of their content
2. **Acknowledge audience**: Show you understand who they create for
3. **Gift-first**: Lead with sending product, NOT asking for content
4. **Low pressure**: "If you love it" framing
5. **Warm + human**: Sound like a real person

## Template Structure
```
Subject: [handle] — [personalized hook, 5-8 words]

Hi [Name or @handle],

[Personalized opening — reference specific content]

[Why the product fits their content niche]

[Gift-first offer — no strings attached]

[Brief brand intro — 1 sentence]

[Close — warm, no pressure]
```

## Anti-Patterns
- ❌ "We'd like to collaborate" — transactional
- ❌ "In exchange for a post" — demanding
- ❌ "Our brand values align" — generic
- ❌ "We love your content" without specifics — lazy
