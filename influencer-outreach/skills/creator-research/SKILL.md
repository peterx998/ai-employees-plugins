# Creator Research

Research and score a creator/KOL for collaboration potential.

## Fit Score Framework (0-100)

### Audience Fit (30 pts)
- Beauty/skincare niche: +15
- Age 25-45 demographic: +10
- English-speaking primarily: +5

### Engagement Quality (25 pts)
- Engagement rate >3%: full points
- Genuine comments (not bot/spam): +10
- Creator replies to comments: +5

### Content Quality (20 pts)
- Professional quality: +10
- Educational/review style: +5
- Consistent posting: +5

### Brand Alignment (15 pts)
- No competing sponsorships: +10
- Authentic review history: +5

### Reach (10 pts)
- 10K-100K (micro): 10 | 100K-500K (mid): 8 | 500K-1M (macro): 5 | <10K (nano): 7

## Red Flags

| Flag | Deduction |
|------|-----------|
| Controversial content | -20 |
| Fake/bot engagement | -25 |
| Competitor exclusive deals | -30 |
| Inactive >30 days | -15 |
