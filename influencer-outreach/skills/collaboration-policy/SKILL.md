# Collaboration Policy

## Creator Tier Matrix

| Tier | Followers | Product Sent | Compensation | Content Expectation |
|------|-----------|-------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Nano | <10K | Standard kit | Product only | 1 organic post |
| Micro | 10K-100K | Deluxe kit | Product + fee | 1 post + usage rights |
| Mid | 100K-500K | Premium kit | Negotiated fee | 1-2 posts + whitelisting |
| Macro | 500K+ | Custom package | Negotiated | Campaign-based |

## Non-Negotiable Terms
- Must disclose paid/gifted partnership per FTC/ASA guidelines
- Content must not make medical claims
- No "guaranteed results" language
