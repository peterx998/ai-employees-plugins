# Distributor Fit Score

## Scoring Framework (0-100)

### Market Coverage (30 pts)
- Existing distribution in target country: +20
- Beauty/skincare channel experience: +10

### Regulatory Capability (25 pts)
- Experience with medical device registration: +15
- In-country regulatory contact: +10

### Portfolio Fit (20 pts)
- Complementary (beauty devices, skincare): +15
- No direct competitor: +5

### Infrastructure (15 pts)
- Warehousing in country: +10
- After-sales service capability: +5

### Financial (10 pts)
- Established company (>2 years): +5
- Verifiable track record: +5
