# Product Policy

## Buyer Type Classification

| Type | Signal | MOQ | Pricing Tier |
|------|--------|-----|-------------|
| Retail (single store) | "For my shop" | 5-10 units | Tier C |
| Clinic (medical practice) | "My clinic", medical license | 10-50 units | Tier B |
| Distributor (regional) | "Distribute in [country]" | 100+ units | Tier A |
| Procurement (chain/hospital) | Formal RFP, procurement dept | 500+ units | Volume |

## Red Flags
- Inquiry from sanctioned country → escalate
- Request to remove/alter certifications → decline
- Request to white-label without registration → escalate
