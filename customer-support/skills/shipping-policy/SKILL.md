# Shipping Policy

| Destination | Standard | Express | Free Shipping Threshold |
|-------------|----------|---------|------------------------|
| US | 5-10 business days | 2-4 days | Orders > $99 |
| Canada | 7-14 business days | 3-6 days | Orders > $149 |
| Mexico | 10-20 business days | 5-10 days | Orders > $199 |
| EU/UK | 7-14 business days | 3-7 days | Orders > €99 |

## Customs & Duties
- **US**: No duties for orders under $800 (de minimis)
- **Canada**: Duties + GST/HST may apply — customer responsible
- **EU/UK**: VAT collected at checkout (IOSS)

## Delayed/Lost Packages
- **Delayed**: Wait 3 more business days, then contact carrier
- **Stuck in customs**: Normal — can take 3-7 extra days
- **Lost**: Reship or refund after carrier confirms loss
