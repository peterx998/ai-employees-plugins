# Refund & Return Policy

| Region | Return Window | Restocking Fee | Device Warranty |
|--------|--------------|----------------|-----------------|
| US | 30 days | 15% if opened | 1 year |
| Canada | 30 days | None (consumer law) | 1 year |
| Mexico | 15 days | 20% if opened | 6 months |
| EU/UK | 14 days (cooling-off) | None | 2 years (statutory) |

## Refund Process
1. Customer contacts support with order # and reason
2. Agent verifies return eligibility
3. Agent issues RMA # and return instructions
4. Warehouse inspects (3-5 business days)
5. Refund issued (5-10 business days)

## Not Refundable
- Opened needle cartridges or serums (hygiene)
- Products past return window
- Products damaged by misuse
